Margin vs markup — what's the difference?
Margin (on revenue)(Price − COGS) / Price
Markup (on cost)(Price − COGS) / COGS
COGS Rp 20.000 → price Rp 80.00075% margin · 300% markup · 4×
4× multiplier= 75% margin = 300% markup
This only covers raw material COGS — not your full business costs.Also account for: packaging design (5–10%), marketing (10–20%), platform fees (3–8%), shipping (Rp 10–25K/order), your time. Aim for at least 70% gross margin on COGS to remain profitable after overhead. Below 60% and you may look busy but not actually be profitable.
